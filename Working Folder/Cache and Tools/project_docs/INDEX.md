# Troy School District Check Registers

Source: BoardDocs (https://go.boarddocs.com/mi/troysd/Board.nsf/Public)
Pulled: 2026-04-26
Files: 45 monthly check registers, FY2023 through FY2026-to-date

Each PDF was attached to the "Treasurer's Report" agenda item of the corresponding
Regular Board of Education meeting. Filenames are prefixed with the meeting date
(YYYY-MM-DD) for sortability; the period the register actually covers is named
inside each PDF (typically the meeting date minus ~2 months).

## Coverage by fiscal year
- FY2023 (Jul 2022 – Jun 2023): 12 registers
- FY2024 (Jul 2023 – Jun 2024): 11 registers
- FY2025 (Jul 2024 – Jun 2025): 12 registers
- FY2026 (Jul 2025 – present): 10 registers

## Notes / known gaps
- The first two files (2022-07-26 and 2022-08-16) were approved at FY23 meetings
  but cover FY22 activity (May 2022 and June+year-end FY22 periods). Keep them
  for context or drop them depending on whether you want activity-by-fiscal-year
  or approved-by-fiscal-year.
- "Periods 12 and 13" files contain June activity plus FY closing entries.
- No October 2023 standalone register was posted to BoardDocs. The Jan 2024
  Regular Meeting approved the "Nov 2023" register; there appears to have been
  no November 2023 Regular Meeting (no agenda matching that pattern was found).
